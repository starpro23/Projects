Who We Are

We are a proudly Kenyan transport company offering reliable matatu travel and parcel delivery services across the country. Based in Mainstage, Meru, our goal is to connect communities through safe, affordable, and efficient transportation.

Whether you're commuting to work, traveling upcountry, or sending a package across town, we are committed to delivering trusted service with a personal touch.

Our Mission

To transform public transport in Kenya by providing:

  • Consistent, reliable matatu rides
  • Fast and secure parcel delivery
  • Customer-first service that respects your time and safety

We aim to support everyday Kenyans while celebrating the vibrant spirit of matatu culture.

Our Services

Passenger Transport

Enjoy fast, affordable, and safe matatu rides across Kenya. We connect towns, cities, and rural areas through reliable SACCO networks.

Parcel Delivery

Send and receive packages quickly and securely. We offer same-day delivery options within towns and upcountry drop-offs at scheduled stops.

Private Hire

Hire a full matatu or van for private group travel, events, or corporate use. Our vehicles are well-maintained and come with experienced drivers.
